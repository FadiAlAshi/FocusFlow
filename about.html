About Us

The story of FocusFlow — a student companion for productivity

The Problem We Solve

Many university students struggle with distraction and difficulty managing time across subjects and assignments. Long hours of studying often yield little real benefit due to a lack of planning and focus.

Our Solution

We built FocusFlow as a simple yet powerful tool that combines:

  • The scientifically-proven Pomodoro technique for focus
  • Smart task management with priorities and due dates
  • Statistics that motivate you to keep going
  • A clean design that works on every device

Our Tech Stack

The site was built entirely using core web technologies we studied in the CSWD1103 course:

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Responsive Design LocalStorage

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data stored securely?

Yes, all data is stored locally in your browser only (LocalStorage) and is never sent to any external server.

Is the website free?

Yes, FocusFlow is completely free and open for all students to use.

Does it work on mobile?

Absolutely! The site is built with Responsive Design and works on all screen sizes.

How can I contribute to the project?

Reach out via the form above — we'd love to hear your ideas and suggestions.
