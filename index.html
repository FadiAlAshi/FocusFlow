Smart Study Tips

Scientifically proven techniques to improve your focus and academic performance

  1. The 25/5 Rule

    Study with full focus for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat to maximize productivity.

  2. Distraction-Free Zone

    Mute phone notifications and remove anything that breaks your attention before starting.

  3. Stay Hydrated

    Dehydration significantly reduces focus — keep a water bottle by your side at all times.

  4. Active Recall Review

    Test yourself instead of just re-reading — it strengthens long-term memory retention.
