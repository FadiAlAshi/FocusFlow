How does the Pomodoro Technique work?

  1. Choose a task and write it in the field above.
  2. Start the timer and work with full focus for 25 minutes.
  3. Take a short break of 5 minutes after each session.
  4. Every 4 sessions, take a long break (15 minutes) to recharge.
